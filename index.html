About

Farmadelica Sound is a full-service recording facility located in Bokeelia, FL on Pine Island, just 10 miles from the mainland. We provide artists a safe, nurturing environment to create to the best of their ability. We cross all genres. We’re about collaboration. We’re about the MUSIC. We offer the following (and more): Production, Arranging, Recording, Mixing, Mastering, Original Music

Staff

Howard Merlin
Founder/Owner, Farmadelica Sound & Cinder Cone Records

A 30+ year veteran of the entertainment business, Merlin’s roles have run the gamut, including stints in recording studios (worked with Elliot Scheiner [Steely Dan], Hall & Oates, Deep Purple, Chris Jasper (Isley Brothers) and many more. He was the Sales/Marketing Director for Earache, Rawkus, ILS/PolyGram, K7 Distribution, Def Jux, 75 Ark and his own Cinder Cone Records. He’s also a Producer/Engineer with 30+ years experience & a Composer/Arranger with multiple projects in the works.

He resides in SW Florida with his wife, media maven Lainie Sevante’ and has launched a new recording studio facility with his partner Bill Wright, called Farmadelica Sound. Tucked away on a Palm Tree Farm in Bokeelia, FL (Pine Island), Farmadelica is a creator’s paradise; sprawling and private, with access to island-hopping galore, boating and the best seafood in North America.
